What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the knowledge about knowing yourself, your emotions and feelings, your state of mind when facing challenges or different situations. It is also about understanding others emotions and respond accordingly. Emotional intelligence is crucial for us when navigating in the uncertain world.

Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence can improve performance, for both individuals and the team. It can also help people cooperate, influence others and make a positive impact to the wider community. Therefore, leading to a happier and meaningful life.
